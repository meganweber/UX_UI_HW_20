the dirt

taking the headache out of travel activity planning

Traveling in large groups can be hectic. We conducted user interviews of those who have traveled with family to uncover a pain point that our app could solve. That information was synthesized into affinity diagram & empathy map to uncover the problem:

Group travelers on the go need to be able to feel empowered when making decisions about activities because it can be difficult to find activities that everyone will enjoy.

Meeting the challenge of pleasing a diverse group of people is tough. We set out to deliver an app that...
  • Delivers immersive insight from travelers and locals
  • Supports multiple, thematic preferences to yeild people pleasing results

Conducted Guerilla Testing to understand how people would search for activities. Testing yielded great insights and improvements:
  • Ability to add favorites using a heart icon
  • Additional confirmations when an action had been taken
  • Added location services
  • More definition between local and traveler reviews
